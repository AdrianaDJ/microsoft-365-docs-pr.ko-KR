1. <span data-ttu-id="d026c-107">전역 관리자 자격 증명을 사용하여 [Microsoft 365 Business](https://portal.office.com)에 로그인합니다.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d026c-107">Sign in to [Microsoft 365 Business](https://portal.office.com) with global admin credentials.</span></span>
